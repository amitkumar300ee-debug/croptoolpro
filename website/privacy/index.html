Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

CropToolPro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the CropToolPro mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

CropToolPro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All file processing (PDF, images) is performed entirely on your device.

2. Data Storage

All files processed by the App remain on your device. We do not have access to your files, documents, photos, or any content you process using the App.

  • PDF files are processed locally on your device
  • Images are processed locally on your device
  • Scanned documents are saved only to your device storage
  • App settings are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect: Advertising ID, device information, general location (country/city). See Google Privacy Policy.

Google ML Kit

Used for OCR (text recognition) and document scanning. Processing happens on-device. No document content is sent to Google servers. See ML Kit Terms.

Google Play Services

Standard Google Play Services for app functionality. See Google Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

CropToolPro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data on our servers, there is no risk of data breach from our end. All your files remain on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
